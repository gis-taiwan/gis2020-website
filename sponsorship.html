第一銀行

First Bank has dedicated in Financial technology since 2013. In recent years, "digital transformation" has been the bank’s main focus, and it has integrated virtual and physical channels actively. At the same time, the bank’s outstanding performance on the business and financial innovation makes it "the best bank of Taiwan in 2017", selected by The Banker Magazine.

In physical channels, 20 digital branches, 49 digital banks experience zones, positioning device iBeacon, and wireless network iTaiwan will be constructed. Furthermore, fingerprint identification, video teller machines (VTM), intelligent robot Pepper, AI intelligent customer service will also be introduced, providing customers all-round financial consulting and product marketing service.

In virtual channels, up-to-date services such as FACE ID mobile banking, AI intelligent customer service "small e", SpeedLoan, intelligent real estate appraisal, and WeChat Payment are also launched by First Bank. Furthermore, the bank’s facebook official fan group has more than 650 thousand fans, which has become the leader among official fan groups in Taiwan’s finance companies. Aside from the official fan group, First Bank also promote their brand value and brand image via community platforms like Line, Instagram, and Youtube.

So far, First bank will proceed with digital transformation and take advantage of AI services as well as new technology to strengthen front desk services. Special branches will also be established to draw more customers.

Deloitte

“Deloitte” is the brand under which tens of thousands of dedicated professionals in independent firms throughout the world collaborate to provide audit, consulting, financial advisory, risk advisory, tax and related services to select clients.These firms are members of Deloitte Touche Tohmatsu Limited, a UK private company limited by guarantee (“DTTL”).

The professional services provided by Deloitte Taiwan include timely updates on regulatory changes, enterprise risk management, integrated financial advisory services, and solutions to enhance competitive strength. Deloitte Taiwan holds a significant position in the market and provides globalized services and resources to help its clients in overseas IPOs, fund raising, listing in Taiwan by foreign issuers, IFRS compliance, China investment services, etc.

PwC

PwC Taiwan was established in 1970 under the name Chen & Chu. The firm grew rapidly over the ensuing decades and now has 8 offices, 92 partners and more than 2,700 distinguished and dedicated business professionals. We provide industry-focused assurance, tax and advisory services to enhance value for our clients.

PwC refers to the network of member firms of PwC International Limited. Across this network, more than 180,000 people in 158 countries share their thinking, experience and solutions to develop fresh perspectives and practical advice. With PwC’s global resources at its disposal, PwC Taiwan follows the world’s major industries constantly, helping clients tap into the latest economic trends and industry developments.

KPMG

KPMG member firms can be found in 153 countries, operating as a global network of independent member firms offering audit, tax and advisory services; working closely with clients, helping them to mitigate risks and grasp opportunities.Member firms' clients include business corporations, governments and public sector agencies and not-for-profit organizations. They look to KPMG for a consistent standard of service based on high order professional capabilities, industry insight and local knowledge.

KPMG in Taiwan employees more than 2500 people, and the firm can be found in Taipei, Hsinchu, Taichung, Tainan, Kaohsiung and Pingtung. KPMG in Taiwan is one of the most considerable accounting firm which provides professional advisory service.
