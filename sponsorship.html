中央研究院永續發展中心

Academia Sinica, the most preeminent academic institution of the Republic of China (Taiwan), was founded to promote and undertake scholarly research in the sciences and humanities. In addition to academic exploration, scientists in Academia Sinica aware of their social responsibility to provide sound scientific evidence and information to solve some of key global sustainability problems that threat the survival of the human society.

As a result, the Center for Sustainability Science (CSS) was established in 2012 to promote researches and activities that strive for the sustainability of the Earth system and human society. Our core missions include:

  1. To plan, organize, and promote Sustainability Science Research Program, which provides funding on integrated researches that are problem-solving oriented, interdisciplinary cooperation approach, and solution-oriented.
  2. To host as a project office of three sustainability science-related international cooperation programs (i.e., IRDR-ICoE, Future Earth, Belmont Forum) and to bridge Taiwan’s researchers with the global sustainability research community.
  3. To provide evidence-based policy recommendations on significant sustainability issues.
台復新創學會

The Tai-Fu Startup Association was formed on July 2016 and is jointly initiated by alumni members of NTU EMBA and NTU Fudan EMBA. The platform is open to all teachers, alumni and current students of NTU and Shanghai Fudan University and makes up the primary members of the association. Its main areas of focus are innovation, creativity, entrepreneurial platforms, as well as organising events with outstanding alumni and professors to share their valuable experiences. This combines academic and practical knowledge and provides a more comprehensive guide for entrepreneurs.

The Tai-Fu Startup Association aims to to research, develop and promote new ideas, and to elevate Taiwan's economy and market value.

In order to provide more career choices and a larger market for new ventures, the Tai-Fu Startup Association has gathered global innvoation resources from Estonia, Russia, France, the United Kingdom, Switzerland (IMD Business School) and Austria. We have also established chapters in Japan, Vietnam, Malaysia, Singapore and Russia, helping members to branch out to other markets.

陳文成博士紀念基金會

陳文成，1950年出生於台北縣林口，畢業於台大數學系和數學研究所，赴美深造，取得密西根大學數學博士學位並任教於卡內基美隆大學，1981年夏，自美國返回台灣探親，7月2日被警備總部約談，次日即陳屍於台大研究生圖書館（即今日的圖資系系館）旁空地上。陳文成命案幾乎可確定為政治謀殺，但和1980年的林宅血案，至今尚未破案，兇手及主謀者仍逍遙法外。

基金會的成立是紀念陳文成活動的一環，並延續其精神，包含追究真相、追求轉型正義、人權的關懷等陳博士理想主義精神的延續。因此藉由出版刊物、舉辦展覽、講座、音樂會、學術研討會、辦理青年歷史營隊，和陳文成統計科學獎學金等不同形式，傳承並發揚其精神遺產。

近年來，在台灣大學的同學師生以及社會的努力與關懷之下，終於在台大的校園內成立了「陳文成事件紀念廣場」，為台灣校園內的轉型正義，樹立了新的里程碑。

期待在眾人的努力之下，台灣的轉型正義工程，能夠繼續進行，使台灣成為一個公平正義，進步民主的國家。
